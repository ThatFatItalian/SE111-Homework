Star Wars - A New Hope

Anthony Palazio 4/21/26

A New Hope is a fun and important sci-fi movie that still holds up today. It follows Luke Skywalker as he gets fights against the Empire and ends up joining rebels trying to destroy the Death Star. The characters are memorable, especially Han Solo, Leia, and Darth Vader, and the story is simple but engaging. The action scenes are exciting, and the music is really iconic. Overall, its a classic that helped shape modern movies.
