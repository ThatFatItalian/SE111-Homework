Star Wars - the empire strikes back

Anthony Palazio 4/21/26

the empire strikes back is one of the strongest star wars movies because it feels bigger and more serious than the first one. the story follows luke as he learns from yoda while han and leia try to escape the empire. the action is great, the characters grow more, and the ending is one of the most famous twists in movie history. it is a classic sequel that many people see as the best in the series.
